About ModX Aero

ModX Aero (OPC) Private Limited is a technology-driven Drone-as-a-Service (DaaS) company delivering safe, efficient, and accurate aerial inspection, survey, and monitoring solutions.

We help government, PSU, industrial, and private sector clients replace traditional high-risk inspection methods with drone-based data collection and analytics-ready reporting.
